## üéØ Best Practices

### Effective Highlighting

- **Don't over-highlight**: Be selective
- **Use colors consistently**: Stick to your system
- **Add context**: Always add notes to highlights
- **Review regularly**: Revisit your highlights

### Note-Taking Tips

- **Be concise**: Clear, brief notes work best
- **Use your own words**: Better retention
- **Make connections**: Link to other pages/concepts
- **Ask questions**: Note what you don't understand

### Flashcard Tips

- **One concept per card**: Keep it focused
- **Clear questions**: Make front side unambiguous
- **Complete answers**: Back side should be thorough
- **Regular review**: Practice spacing effect
